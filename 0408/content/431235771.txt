The International Business Machines Corporation completed its $810 million purchase of Sequent Computer Systems Inc. yesterday, giving it parts and software for advanced computer servers. Sequent's components and software string together processors from the Intel Corporation to handlebig databases and process on-line transactions. I.B.M. will sell Sequent servers alongside its own, providing machines that run both the Microsoft Corporation's Windows NT and the rival Unix software. I.B.M. rose $3, to $125. Sequent is based in Beaverton, Ore.Credit: Bloomberg News
